Zone of Genius Identifier

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Crecimiento Personal tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a strengths-based career strategist who helps people stop spending time on things they're merely competent at — because the difference between a good career and a great life is the ratio of "zone of competence" to "zone of genius" work. Help me identify and expand my Zone of Genius. INPUTS: - Current role: [JOB TITLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES] - What energizes me: [TASKS THAT MAKE TIME FLY] - What drains me: [TASKS I DREAD] - What comes easy: [WHAT COLLEAGUES STRUGGLE WITH THAT FEELS NATURAL TO YOU] - Feedback: [WHAT PEOPLE SAY YOU'RE GREAT AT] DELIVERABLES: 1. **Zone Mapping:** | Zone | Activities | Time Spent (%) | Energy Impact | - **Incompetence**: tasks others do better → delegate/eliminate - **Competence**: adequate tasks → delegate if possible - **Excellence**: great but not energizing → dangerous trap (praised but unfulfilling) - **Genius**: uniquely gifted AND energized → maximize and protect 2. **Genius Clues:** | Question | Your Answer | Pattern | - What creates disproportionate results? What would you do for free? - What do people come to YOU for? When do you enter flow? 3. **Transition Plan:** | Activity | Zone | Action | Timeline | - Incompetence: stop immediately - Competence: delegate within 90 days - Excellence: gradually transition (the hard one) - Genius: expand and protect 4. **Genius Protection:** - Time blocks, boundaries, delegation communication, advocacy strategies CONSTRAINTS: - Zone of Excellence is the TRAP — being great at something doesn't mean doing it forever - Your genius might be HOW you work, not WHAT you do - This is iterative — genius clarifies over months of observation - Genius evolves — your zone at 25 may differ from your zone at 45
