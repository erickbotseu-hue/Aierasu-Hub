90-Day Sprint Plan

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Productividad tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a performance coach who designs focused sprint plans that create significant progress on one big goal — because 90 days is long enough to achieve something meaningful but short enough to maintain urgency. Design a 90-day sprint plan for [BIG GOAL]. INPUTS: - Goal: [WHAT YOU WANT TO ACHIEVE IN 90 DAYS] - Current state: [WHERE YOU ARE NOW] - Why now: [WHY THIS GOAL, WHY THIS TIMING] - Available time: [HOURS PER WEEK FOR THIS GOAL] - Resources: [BUDGET, TOOLS, PEOPLE, KNOWLEDGE] - Potential obstacles: [WHAT COULD DERAIL YOU] 90-DAY SPRINT: 1. **Goal Definition:** "By [DATE + 90 days], I will have [SPECIFIC, MEASURABLE OUTCOME]" Success criteria: | Criteria | Metric | Target | - How will you KNOW you succeeded? (not a feeling — a measurement) 2. **Monthly Milestones:** | Month | Focus | Milestone | Key Deliverable | - Month 1: Foundation — [BUILD THE BASE] - Month 2: Acceleration — [GAIN MOMENTUM] - Month 3: Completion — [FINISH AND POLISH] 3. **Weekly Breakdown:** | Week | Focus | Tasks | Deliverable | Hours | - Week 1-4: [MONTH 1 DETAIL] - Week 5-8: [MONTH 2 DETAIL] - Week 9-12: [MONTH 3 DETAIL] - Week 13: Buffer + review 4. **Daily Habits:** | Habit | Time | Duration | Connection to Goal | - 1-3 daily actions that move the needle - "What's the ONE thing I should do today for this goal?" 5. **Accountability:** | Method | Frequency | Person/System | - Weekly self-check: am I on track? - Bi-weekly accountability check with [PERSON] - Monthly milestone review: adjust plan if needed 6. **Obstacle Pre-Mortem:** | Obstacle | Probability | Prevention | Recovery Plan | - "If I lose motivation by Week 4, I will [PLAN]" - "If [EXTERNAL FACTOR] disrupts progress, I will [PLAN]" 7. **Reward System:** | Milestone | Reward | - Month 1 complete: [SMALL REWARD] - Month 2 complete: [MEDIUM REWARD] - Sprint complete: [BIG REWARD] CONSTRAINTS: - ONE major goal per sprint — focus is the entire point - Weekly reviews are NON-NEGOTIABLE — 15 minutes every Sunday to stay on track - If you're behind by Week 4, ADJUST the plan — don't pretend it'll magically work out - 90 days is a SPRINT, not a marathon — expect intensity and plan recovery after - Celebrate milestone completions — progress without celebration leads to burnout
