Act as a slam poetry champion who turns raw emotion into electric performance — because spoken word isn't just poetry you read aloud, it's a different art form entirely. Write a spoken word piece about [TOPIC] for performance. INPUTS: - Topic: [SUBJECT MATTER] - Tone: [PASSIONATE / PROVOCATIVE / HEALING / DEFIANT / TENDER / RAGE] - Length: [2-4 MINUTES WHEN SPOKEN — roughly 250-500 words] - Audience: [OPEN MIC / COMPETITION / RALLY / CLASSROOM / INTIMATE] - Personal connection: [HOW THIS TOPIC RELATES TO YOU — or create a persona] SPOKEN WORD PIECE: 1. **Opening (first 10 seconds):** - A line that makes the room go quiet - Establishes tone and urgency immediately - Can be a question, a declaration, a challenge, or an image 2. **Body (build and build):** - Repetition/refrain: a returning phrase that gains meaning each time - Lists that accelerate: building rhythm and emotional intensity - Imagery from LIVED EXPERIENCE, not abstractions - Strategic shifts: quiet moments before loud ones, humor before pain 3. **Performance Directions:** Include [BRACKETS] for delivery: - [pause — let it land] - [louder, building] - [whisper — pull them in] - [faster — urgency] - [slow — each word separate] - [to one person in the audience] 4. **Climax:** - The loudest, most raw, most REAL moment - Everything builds to this — it's the reason the poem exists - Often the most personal revelation 5. **Closing:** - A gut-punch ending or a quiet exhale - Circles back to the opening (transformed) - The last line should hang in the room after you've stopped speaking CRAFT ELEMENTS: - Word play: puns, double meanings, sound play - Internal rhyme and assonance: for rhythm without sing-song - Alliteration: for emphasis and punch - Repetition: escalating meaning with each return - Code-switching: shift registers for effect CONSTRAINTS: - Written for the EAR, not the page — if it reads better than it sounds, it's an essay - The body is 50% of the work; voice, pacing, and silence are the other 50% - Personal stories > political statements — specificity creates connection - Vulnerability is POWER in spoken word — don't hide behind cleverness - End STRONG — the last 10 seconds matter more than anything in the middle
