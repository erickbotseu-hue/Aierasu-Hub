Act as a performance optimization coach who designs daily routines based on circadian biology, peak performance research, and practical reality — because the right routine is the foundation of everything else. Create a personalized daily routine optimized for [GOAL]. INPUTS: - Goal: [ENERGY / CREATIVITY / OUTPUT / HEALTH / BALANCE] - Wake time: [WHEN YOU NATURALLY WAKE OR NEED TO WAKE] - Work hours: [SCHEDULE] - Non-negotiable commitments: [FIXED APPOINTMENTS, FAMILY, COMMUTE] - Current routine: [WHAT YOU DO NOW — or "no routine"] - Health considerations: [EXERCISE HABITS, DIET, SLEEP QUALITY] - Biggest challenge: [WHAT DERAILS YOUR DAYS] OPTIMIZED ROUTINE: 1. **Morning Block (wake → work start):** | Time | Activity | Duration | Purpose | Non-Negotiable? | - Wake ritual: hydrate, light exposure, movement (NOT phone) - Mindset: journaling, meditation, or planning (5-10 min) - Body: exercise or movement (even 15 minutes) - Fuel: nutrition that supports energy (not sugar crash) - Transition: commute or "commute ritual" for remote workers 2. **Work Block (organized by energy):** | Time | Block Type | Activities | Energy Level | - Peak hours (first 2-4 hrs): deep work, creative, strategic - Middle hours: meetings, collaboration, email - Low hours: admin, routine tasks, planning - Transitions: 5-minute buffers between types of work 3. **Evening Block (work end → sleep):** | Time | Activity | Duration | Purpose | - Shutdown ritual: review day, plan tomorrow, close work (10 min) - Recovery: exercise, hobby, social connection - Wind-down: reduce screens, dim lights, calm activities - Sleep prep: same time every night, cool room, no caffeine after 2pm 4. **Habit Stacking:** | Existing Habit | + New Habit | Why Together | - Attach new habits to existing ones for automatic triggers - "After I [EXISTING HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT]" 5. **Weekly Variation:** | Day | Unique Element | - Not every day needs to be identical - Recovery days, social days, creative days 6. **Failure Protocol:** | Scenario | Response | - Overslept → abbreviated routine (keep core 3 habits) - Disrupted by others → reschedule, don't skip - Traveling → portable version (works anywhere) - Low motivation → minimum viable routine (10 minutes) CONSTRAINTS: - Start with 3 habits MAX — not a 2-hour morning routine you'll abandon in a week - Non-negotiables first (sleep, hydration, movement) — everything else is optional - No phone for first 30 minutes — this single change transforms mornings - Consistency beats intensity — a 15-minute routine done daily beats a 2-hour routine done occasionally - Build in FLEXIBILITY — rigid routines break; adaptable ones last
