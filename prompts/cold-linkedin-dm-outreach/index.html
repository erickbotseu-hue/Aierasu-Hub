Cold LinkedIn DM Outreach

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a LinkedIn social selling expert who books meetings through DMs — not by spamming pitches, but by starting genuine conversations that naturally lead to business. Write a cold LinkedIn DM outreach sequence for [SERVICE]. INPUTS: - Service: [WHAT YOU OFFER] - Target: [JOB TITLE AT COMPANY TYPE] - Value proposition: [THE #1 BENEFIT YOU PROVIDE] - Price range: $[AMOUNT] - Social proof: [RESULTS YOU'VE ACHIEVED] - Your LinkedIn profile: [OPTIMIZED? WHAT'S YOUR HEADLINE?] 3-MESSAGE SEQUENCE: **Message 1 — Connection Request (300 char max):** "Hey [NAME], saw your [SPECIFIC POST/ARTICLE/ROLE]. [GENUINE OBSERVATION — not flattery]. I work with [SIMILAR COMPANIES] on [RELATED TOPIC]. Would love to connect." DO: reference something specific from their profile DON'T: pitch in the connection request (kills acceptance rate) **Message 2 — Day 2-3 (after accepted):** "Thanks for connecting, [NAME]! Quick question — are you dealing with [SPECIFIC CHALLENGE] at [COMPANY]? I ask because we recently helped [SIMILAR COMPANY] [SPECIFIC RESULT], and I noticed you might be facing something similar based on [OBSERVATION]. No pitch — just curious if it resonates." DO: ask a relevant question, share a relevant insight DON'T: send a paragraph about your company and services **Message 3 — Day 5-7 (if no reply):** "[NAME], not trying to be annoying — I know you're busy. I put together a quick [RESOURCE: case study / guide / video] on [TOPIC RELEVANT TO THEIR ROLE]. Thought it might be useful regardless of whether we chat: [LINK] Either way, happy to have you in my network!" DO: provide value with no strings attached DON'T: pressure for a meeting or get passive-aggressive **If They Respond Positively:** "Glad it resonated! Would a quick 15-minute call make sense? I can share exactly how we approached [SPECIFIC CHALLENGE] for [SIMILAR COMPANY]. Here's my calendar: [LINK] — or just tell me a time that works." **If They Say "Not Now":** "Totally get it — timing is everything. Mind if I check back in [TIMEFRAME]? Happy to share relevant content in the meantime." **Pre-Outreach Engagement (1-2 weeks before DM):** | Action | Frequency | Goal | - Like their posts: 3-5 times - Leave thoughtful comments: 2-3 times (not "Great post!") - Share their content with your insight added - This warms them up before you ever send a message CONSTRAINTS: - NEVER pitch in the connection request — it's the #1 reason people decline - NEVER send paragraph-length messages — keep under 100 words - NEVER use "I'd love to pick your brain" (it's a red flag for an incoming pitch) - Personalize EVERY message — LinkedIn users can spot copy-paste templates instantly - LinkedIn limits connection requests: stay under 100 per week to avoid restrictions - Your PROFILE matters more than your outreach — optimize headline, about, and banner first
