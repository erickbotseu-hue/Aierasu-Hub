Act as a customer success strategist who has reduced churn from 8% to 2% at a B2B SaaS company. You know that retention is the most profitable growth lever — a 5% reduction in churn can increase profits by 25-95%. Design a comprehensive retention strategy for [BUSINESS]. INPUTS: - Business type: [SaaS / E-COMMERCE / SUBSCRIPTION / SERVICE] - Current churn rate: [X% MONTHLY] - Current retention rate: [X%] - Average customer lifecycle: [MONTHS] - ARPU: $[AMOUNT] - LTV: $[AMOUNT] - Customer base: [NUMBER] - Top churn reason (if known): [REASON] - Target churn rate: [Y%] RETENTION STRATEGY: 1. **Churn Analysis Framework:** - Churn segmentation: who's churning? (by segment, tenure, plan, acquisition channel) - Churn timeline: when do most customers leave? (month 1? month 6? at renewal?) - Churn reasons taxonomy: | Reason | % of Churn | Preventable? | Intervention | - Revenue churn vs. logo churn: which matters more for your business? 2. **Onboarding Optimization (Month 1 — highest risk period):** - Time-to-value target: get new customer to "aha moment" within [X] days - Onboarding milestones: 5 key actions that predict long-term retention - Automated onboarding sequence: triggers, messages, and timing - Human touchpoints: when to call, email, or check in personally - Onboarding health score: formula using completion of key milestones 3. **Engagement Loops (Months 2-6):** 5 mechanisms to keep customers actively using and deriving value: | Loop Type | How It Works | Trigger | Expected Impact | - Product usage loops (features that create habits) - Communication loops (check-ins, tips, community) - Value loops (regular proof of ROI or benefit) - Social loops (community, peer learning) - Expansion loops (new features, use cases, users) 4. **Health Score Model:** | Signal | Weight | Healthy | Warning | Critical | Signals to include: - Login frequency - Feature adoption depth - Support ticket volume and sentiment - NPS/CSAT score - Billing issues - Champion engagement (is your internal champion still active?) Score formula: weighted composite → Health Score (0-100) Tier thresholds: Healthy (80+) → Watch (60-79) → At-Risk (40-59) → Critical (0-39) 5. **At-Risk Customer Playbook:** For each risk tier: | Tier | Trigger | Automated Action | Human Action | Timeline | - Watch: gentle nudge + value reminder - At-Risk: CSM outreach + usage review + rescue offer - Critical: executive escalation + retention offer + honest conversation 6. **Expansion Revenue Strategy:** - Upsell triggers: when a customer is READY for more (usage-based signals) - Cross-sell opportunities: complementary products/features - Pricing tiers that create natural upgrade paths - Target: [X]% of revenue from expansion by Quarter [Y] 7. **Loyalty & Advocacy Program:** - Loyalty tiers with specific benefits per tier - Referral program with incentive structure - Customer advisory board invitation criteria - Case study and testimonial collection automation 8. **Win-Back Campaign (recovering churned customers):** - Re-engagement sequence: 3 emails over 30 days - Win-back offer: what to offer and when - Feedback collection: exit survey → product improvement loop - Success rate benchmark: 5-15% of churned customers recoverable 9. **Metrics Dashboard:** | Metric | Current | Target | Tracking Method | - Monthly churn rate (logo and revenue) - Net revenue retention (NRR) — target: 110%+ - Customer health score distribution - Time-to-value (new customers) - NPS trend - Expansion revenue % CONSTRAINT: Retention starts BEFORE the customer signs up — with proper expectation setting during sales. If your sales team overpromises, no amount of CSM effort will fix churn. Address the root cause, not just the symptoms.
