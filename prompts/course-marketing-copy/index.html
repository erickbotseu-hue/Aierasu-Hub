Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as an online education marketer who has launched courses generating $500K+ in revenue. You know that selling a course is selling a transformation, not information. Write complete marketing copy for an online course: "[COURSE TITLE]." INPUTS: - Course topic: [TOPIC] - Target student: [WHO — be specific about their situation] - Price: $[PRICE] - Course length: [X hours / modules] - Key transformation: [WHAT THEY'LL BE ABLE TO DO AFTER] - Instructor credibility: [RELEVANT EXPERIENCE OR RESULTS] DELIVER: 1. **Sales Page Copy:** - Pre-headline: "For [AUDIENCE] who [SITUATION]" - Headline: Promise the transformation, not the information - Sub-headline: How + timeline + differentiator - Hero section CTA button text 2. **Problem Agitation Section (200 words):** - 3 specific pain points in THEIR language - Cost of NOT learning this (money, time, career impact) - "Sound familiar?" moment of recognition 3. **Curriculum Overview:** FOR EACH MODULE: catchy name + "By the end, you'll be able to [OUTCOME]" 4. **Instructor Bio (100 words):** Results-focused, not resume-focused. What have you DONE, not where did you study. 5. **FAQ Objection Handlers (6 questions):** Each secretly addresses: too expensive, no time, already know this, will it work for me, can I get a refund, how is this different 6. **Social Proof Section:** - 3 testimonial templates (results-driven, not "great course!") - Student transformation stats: "Over [X] students enrolled, [Y]% completion rate" 7. **Pricing Section:** - Value stack (individual values totaling 5-10x the price) - Guarantee: specific, generous, risk-reversing - Urgency: limited enrollment, early bird, bonus deadline 8. **Launch Emails (3):** - Email 1: Story + announcement - Email 2: Curriculum reveal + testimonial - Email 3: Last chance + urgency CONSTRAINT: Sell the OUTCOME, not the curriculum. Nobody wants "12 modules and 47 videos." They want "the confidence to [RESULT] within [TIMEFRAME]."
