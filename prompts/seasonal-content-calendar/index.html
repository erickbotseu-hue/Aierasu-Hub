Seasonal Content Calendar

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a content strategist who aligns content with buying cycles, cultural moments, and industry events — so you publish the right content at the right time. Create a seasonal content calendar for [BUSINESS] in [INDUSTRY]. INPUTS: - Business: [WHAT YOU SELL] - Industry: [YOUR MARKET] - Content channels: [BLOG / SOCIAL / EMAIL / VIDEO / PODCAST] - Team capacity: [PIECES PER WEEK] - Buying cycle: [WHEN DO CUSTOMERS BUY MOST/LEAST] - Geographic market: [US / GLOBAL / SPECIFIC REGIONS] CONTENT CALENDAR: 1. **Annual Overview:** | Month | Industry Event | Cultural Moment | Buying Cycle Phase | Content Theme | Map all 12 months with key dates, events, and content themes. 2. **Content Categories:** | Category | % of Content | Purpose | Examples | - Evergreen educational (40%): SEO-driven, always relevant - Seasonal/timely (25%): tied to events, trends, dates - Product/promotional (20%): launches, features, offers - Community/engagement (15%): UGC, polls, behind-the-scenes 3. **Monthly Deep Dive (template):** | Week | Content Piece | Channel | Topic | Keyword Target | CTA | - Week 1: [CONTENT] - Week 2: [CONTENT] - Week 3: [CONTENT] - Week 4: [CONTENT] 4. **Promotional Calendar:** | Date | Event | Promotion | Content Needed | Lead Time | - Major sale events (Black Friday, end of year, etc.) - Industry-specific dates - Product launches - Content needed: emails, social, landing pages, ads 5. **Content Repurposing Plan:** | Original Content | Repurposed Formats | - Blog post → social thread → email → infographic → video - Webinar → blog recap → clip series → email course - Case study → social proof posts → ad creative → sales deck CONSTRAINTS: - Plan content at least 4 weeks in advance — last-minute content is mediocre content - Seasonal content must be published BEFORE the season (not during — too late for SEO) - Balance evergreen and timely content — 60/40 ratio minimum - Every piece must have a clear BUSINESS purpose — no content for content's sake - Review and adjust quarterly based on performance data
