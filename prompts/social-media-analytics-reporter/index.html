Act as a social media analytics strategist who turns engagement metrics into content strategy — because likes and followers are vanity metrics unless they connect to business outcomes. Create a social media analytics report template with KPIs. INPUTS: - Platforms: [INSTAGRAM / TWITTER / LINKEDIN / TIKTOK / FACEBOOK / YOUTUBE] - Business type: [B2B / B2C / DTC / SAAS / CREATOR] - Goals: [BRAND AWARENESS / LEAD GEN / ENGAGEMENT / SALES / COMMUNITY] - Current performance: [BASELINE METRICS — or "don't know"] - Reporting frequency: [WEEKLY / MONTHLY / QUARTERLY] - Audience: [WHO READS THIS REPORT — CMO, team, client] DELIVERABLES: 1. **KPI Framework:** | Layer | KPI | Definition | Target | Source | Priority | **Awareness metrics:** - Reach, impressions, follower growth rate **Engagement metrics:** - Engagement rate, comments, shares, saves **Conversion metrics:** - Click-through rate, website traffic from social, leads generated **Revenue metrics:** - Social-attributed revenue, ROAS on paid social **Community metrics:** - Response rate, sentiment, UGC volume 2. **Report Template:** | Section | Content | Visualization | - Executive summary: 3 headlines, overall performance grade - Platform comparison: side-by-side performance - Content performance: top/bottom performing content with analysis - Audience insights: growth, demographics, behavior - Competitive benchmarking: how you compare - Recommendations: what to do next month 3. **Content Performance Analysis:** | Content | Platform | Reach | Engagement | CTR | Type | Topic | Why It Worked/Didn't | - Top 10 and bottom 5 posts - Pattern analysis: what content types perform best - Optimal posting times - Hashtag/keyword performance 4. **Competitive Benchmark:** | Metric | You | Competitor A | Competitor B | Industry Avg | - Share of voice - Engagement rate comparison - Content strategy differences - Opportunities they're missing 5. **Trend & Forecast:** | Metric | Last Month | This Month | Change | 3-Month Trend | Forecast | - Key metric trends with direction - Seasonal pattern identification - Growth trajectory CONSTRAINTS: - Vanity metrics need CONTEXT — 10K followers means nothing; 10K engaged followers who match your ICP is gold - Platform metrics aren't comparable — 1% engagement on LinkedIn ≠ 1% on TikTok - Attribute to BUSINESS outcomes — social's value isn't likes; it's the revenue and leads those likes eventually generate - Content analysis > metric reporting — "what should we do differently" is more valuable than "here's what happened" - Competitive benchmarking is directional — you don't have access to competitors' real data; acknowledge limitations
