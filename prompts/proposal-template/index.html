INPUTS: - Service: [WHAT YOU PROVIDE] - Client type: [INDUSTRY, SIZE, TYPICAL BUYER] - Average deal size: $[AMOUNT] - Proposal format: [PDF / INTERACTIVE / VIDEO + DOC] - Sales process stage: [WHEN IS THE PROPOSAL SENT — after call? After discovery?] - Competition: [WHO ELSE IS BIDDING] PROPOSAL STRUCTURE: 1. **Cover Page:** - Client name (prominent — this is about THEM) - "Prepared for [CLIENT NAME]" — not "About Us" - Date and validity period - Your logo (smaller than theirs) - Clean, professional design 2. **Executive Summary (1 page max):** 3-paragraph structure: - Paragraph 1: THEIR situation (prove you listened in discovery) - Paragraph 2: The impact of the problem (cost, risk, opportunity loss) - Paragraph 3: Your proposed solution and expected outcome (results first) "We understand that [CLIENT] is facing [CHALLENGE], which is resulting in [IMPACT]. Our proposed [SOLUTION] will deliver [SPECIFIC OUTCOME] within [TIMEFRAME]." 3. **Understanding Your Situation (1-2 pages):** - Restate their challenges using THEIR words from discovery - Show you understand their business context - This section proves you LISTENED — most competitors skip this | Challenge | Impact | Desired Outcome | 4. **Proposed Solution (2-3 pages):** - What you'll deliver (scope, deliverables, milestones) - HOW you'll approach it (methodology, not just outcomes) - Timeline with milestones: | Phase | Duration | Deliverables | Their Involvement | - What's included AND what's explicitly NOT included (scope boundaries) 5. **Why Us (1 page):** | Criteria | Us | Alternative A | Alternative B | - 3-4 differentiators specific to THEIR needs - Relevant case study: similar industry, similar challenge, specific results - Team members who'll work on their project (names, photos, credentials) 6. **Investment (1 page):** 3-tier pricing: | Package | Includes | Price | Best For | - Option 1 (Essential): core deliverables only - Option 2 (Recommended): core + extras (highlight this one) - Option 3 (Premium): everything + ongoing support Why 3 options: anchoring + choice architecture - Payment terms: deposit, milestones, or monthly - ROI justification: "At $[PRICE], this investment pays for itself when you achieve [SPECIFIC METRIC]" 7. **Social Proof:** - 2 case studies relevant to their industry/challenge: | Company | Challenge | Solution | Result | - 2-3 testimonial quotes with names and titles - Client logos (if applicable) 8. **Next Steps:** - Exactly what happens after they say yes - Step 1: Sign agreement (include link or instructions) - Step 2: Kickoff call scheduled within [X] days - Step 3: First deliverable in [X] weeks - Proposal valid until: [DATE] 9. **Appendix:** - Detailed scope of work - Team bios - Terms and conditions - FAQ (address objections preemptively) CONSTRAINTS: - Do NOT lead with your company history — lead with THEIR problem - Do NOT present one price — always offer 3 options (most people pick the middle) - Keep under 10 pages — executives don't read 30-page proposals - Do NOT use jargon — write at the level your audience speaks - Include a clear deadline: "This proposal is valid until [DATE]" — creates urgency - The proposal should be a SUMMARY of the conversation, not new information
