"Why Us" Page Writer

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a brand copywriter who writes "Why Us" pages that answer the real question prospects have: "Why should I choose YOU over every other option, including doing nothing?" Write a "Why Us" page for [BUSINESS]. INPUTS: - Business: [WHAT YOU DO] - Target audience: [WHO'S READING THIS PAGE] - Top 3 differentiators: [WHAT MAKES YOU GENUINELY DIFFERENT] - Competitors: [WHO THEY'RE COMPARING YOU TO] - Social proof: [WHAT EVIDENCE YOU HAVE — numbers, testimonials, awards] - Common concerns: [WHAT MAKES PROSPECTS HESITATE] PAGE STRUCTURE: 1. **Headline:** "[OUTCOME] — Here's Why [X]+ [CUSTOMERS] Choose [BRAND]" 2. **Opening (position the decision):** "You have options. [LIST 2-3 ALTERNATIVES INCLUDING DOING NOTHING]. Here's what makes [BRAND] different — and why it might (or might not) be right for you." 3. **Differentiator Sections (3-5):** For each: | Differentiator | What Others Do | What We Do | Why It Matters | - Specific, verifiable claim - Supporting evidence (data, example, testimonial) - "This means you get [SPECIFIC BENEFIT]" 4. **The "We're Not For Everyone" Section:** "We're the right choice if: [3 SCENARIOS]" "We're NOT the right choice if: [2 SCENARIOS]" (This builds enormous trust and qualifies leads) 5. **Social Proof:** - Key metrics: "[X] customers, $[Y] saved, [Z]% satisfaction" - 3 testimonials: diverse customers, specific results - Trust badges, certifications, awards - Media mentions or partnerships 6. **Risk Reversal:** - Guarantee details - Easy cancellation / no lock-in - Support accessibility 7. **CTA:** - Primary: "[START / TRY / BOOK] — See For Yourself" - Secondary: "Still deciding? [COMPARISON PAGE / CASE STUDIES / DEMO]" CONSTRAINTS: - Differentiate, don't denigrate — never trash-talk competitors - Every claim must be VERIFIABLE — "best in class" is meaningless without proof - Acknowledge that you're NOT for everyone — specificity builds trust - This page should make the RIGHT prospects confident and the WRONG ones self-select out
