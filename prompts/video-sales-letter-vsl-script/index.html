Video Sales Letter (VSL) Script

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a direct-response video copywriter who scripts VSLs that hold attention for 5-10 minutes and convert viewers into buyers — without feeling like an infomercial. Write a Video Sales Letter (VSL) script for [PRODUCT]. INPUTS: - Product: [WHAT IT IS AND WHAT IT DOES] - Price: $[AMOUNT] - Target viewer: [WHO IS WATCHING AND WHERE — ad, landing page, email] - Video length: [5 / 7 / 10 MINUTES] - Visual style: [TALKING HEAD / SLIDES / SCREEN RECORDING / ANIMATED] - Main offer: [WHAT THEY GET WHEN THEY BUY] VSL SCRIPT: 1. **Hook (0:00-0:30):** Open with a bold statement, question, or story that creates instant curiosity: - "[SURPRISING STAT ABOUT THEIR PROBLEM]" - "What if I told you that [COMMON BELIEF] is completely wrong?" - "In the next [X] minutes, I'm going to show you [SPECIFIC OUTCOME]" VISUAL: text on screen reinforcing the hook 2. **Problem (0:30-2:00):** - Describe THEIR daily frustration (use their language) - 3 pain points with emotional weight - "If this sounds familiar, you're not alone — [STAT]" - Make them feel understood, not judged VISUAL: relatable scenarios, b-roll of frustration 3. **Failed Solutions (2:00-3:00):** - "You've probably tried [COMMON SOLUTION A]... and it didn't work because..." - "Or maybe [SOLUTION B]... which fails because..." - "It's not your fault — these approaches are fundamentally flawed" VISUAL: crossing out bad solutions 4. **The Bridge (3:00-3:30):** - "What if there was a way to [DESIRED OUTCOME] without [BIGGEST OBJECTION]?" - Introduce the mechanism/framework (NOT the product yet) VISUAL: breakthrough moment 5. **Product Reveal (3:30-5:00):** - Name and introduce the product - Show exactly what's included - Demo or walkthrough of key features - Social proof: "[X] people already use this" VISUAL: product demo, screenshots, UI walkthrough 6. **Proof (5:00-6:00):** - 2-3 specific customer results with numbers - Video testimonial clips (if available) - Before/after comparison VISUAL: testimonial clips, results screenshots 7. **Offer Stack (6:00-7:30):** - What they get (list with values) - Bonuses (2-3 with dollar values) - Total value vs. actual price - Payment options VISUAL: offer stack building, prices appearing 8. **Risk Reversal (7:30-8:00):** - Guarantee details - "You literally have nothing to lose" VISUAL: guarantee badge 9. **Final CTA (8:00-end):** - "Click the button below to [START/GET/JOIN]" - Repeat the transformation promise - Final urgency element - P.S. moment: one more reason to act now VISUAL: CTA button, countdown (if applicable) PRODUCTION NOTES: - Pacing: vary energy to maintain attention - Text overlays: key points appear on screen (many watch without sound) - Music: subtle background, builds during offer section - Cuts: change visual every 5-7 seconds (prevents attention drop) - Captions: always (80%+ of social video is watched without sound) CONSTRAINTS: - The hook must be COMPELLING in 3 seconds — or they'll scroll/click away - Do NOT spend more than 30% of the video on the pitch — 70% must be value/story - Every claim must be DEMONSTRABLE — show, don't just tell - Script must work WITHOUT audio (text overlays for key points) - End with ONE clear CTA — don't give multiple options
