Content team: [SIZE AND CAPABILITIES] - Budget for tools/links: $[AMOUNT/MONTH] 90-DAY STRATEGY: **Month 1: Technical Foundation + Quick Wins** 1. **Technical SEO Audit:** | Issue | Priority | Impact | Fix | - Site speed: Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID/INP, CLS) — target all "Good" - Mobile usability: responsive design, tap targets, viewport - Crawling: robots.txt, sitemap.xml, orphan pages - Indexation: check Google Search Console for errors - Schema markup: product, article, FAQ, organization - Internal linking: fix broken links, improve link equity flow - HTTPS, canonical tags, hreflang (if multilingual) 2. **Quick Win Keywords (10 targets):** | Keyword | Current Position | Target | Difficulty | Action | - Keywords ranking positions 5-20 (already close to page 1) - Optimize existing pages: title, H1, meta description, content expansion - Internal link to these pages from high-authority pages - Expected impact: 30-50% traffic increase in 30 days **Month 2: Content Engine** 3. **Keyword Research (30 targets):** | Cluster | Primary Keyword | Volume | KD | Secondary Keywords | Content Type | - 3-4 topic clusters with pillar pages + supporting articles - Mix of informational and commercial intent - Long-tail keywords for quick rankings - "People Also Ask" opportunities 4. **Content Plan (15 articles):** For EACH article: | # | Title | Target Keyword | Word Count | Content Type | Funnel Stage | - 5 pillar articles (2,000-4,000 words, comprehensive) - 5 supporting articles (1,000-2,000 words, specific topics) - 3 comparison/alternative articles (commercial intent) - 2 data-driven/original research pieces (linkable assets) Content brief template for each: - Target keyword + LSI keywords - Search intent analysis - Competitor content audit (what's ranking, what can be better) - Outline with H2/H3 structure - Internal linking targets - CTA placement 5. **On-Page Optimization Checklist:** For every page: - Title tag: keyword + benefit (under 60 chars) - Meta description: keyword + CTA (under 155 chars) - H1: one per page, includes primary keyword - URL: short, descriptive, includes keyword - Content: keyword in first 100 words, natural use throughout - Images: alt text, compressed, WebP format - Internal links: 3-5 per article to related content **Month 3: Authority Building** 6. **Link Building Campaign (10 tactics):** | Tactic | Target Links | Difficulty | Timeline | - Guest posting on relevant blogs (3-5 posts) - Broken link building (find and replace dead links) - Resource page outreach - HARO / journalist queries - Original data/study (link magnet) - Unlinked brand mentions → link requests - Partner cross-linking - Podcast appearances (link in show notes) - Tool/calculator creation (linkable asset) - Expert roundup participation 7. **Competitor Gap Analysis:** | Competitor | Keywords They Rank For That You Don't | Estimated Traffic Value | - Content gaps: topics they cover that you don't - Link gaps: sites linking to them but not you - Technical advantages: speed, structure, features 8. **Monthly KPI Targets:** | Month | Organic Traffic | Keywords in Top 10 | Referring Domains | Conversions | - Month 1: +20% traffic from quick wins - Month 2: +15 new keywords ranking - Month 3: +10 referring domains, +40% traffic from Month 0 CONSTRAINTS: - Do NOT buy links — earn them through content quality and outreach - Do NOT keyword stuff — write for humans, optimize for search engines - Do NOT ignore search intent — a perfectly optimized article that doesn't match intent won't rank - Focus on TOPICS, not just keywords — Google understands topical authority - Track CONVERSIONS, not just traffic — 1,000 visitors who don't buy is worse than 100 who do
