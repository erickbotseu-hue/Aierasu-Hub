Customer Onboarding Email Sequence

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Act as a customer success strategist who designs onboarding sequences that get users to their "aha moment" fast — because customers who experience value in the first 7 days stay 10x longer. Create a customer onboarding email sequence for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. INPUTS: - Product: [WHAT THEY JUST PURCHASED OR SIGNED UP FOR] - Aha moment: [THE KEY ACTION THAT CORRELATES WITH RETENTION] - Time to value: [HOW LONG UNTIL THEY EXPERIENCE THE BENEFIT] - Common drop-off points: [WHERE NEW USERS GET STUCK OR QUIT] - Support resources: [HELP CENTER / COMMUNITY / LIVE CHAT / FAQ] - Upsell opportunity: [WHAT YOU'D SELL THEM NEXT] 5-EMAIL ONBOARDING SEQUENCE: **Email 1 — Immediately: "Welcome + Quick Start"** - Subject: "Welcome! Here's your first step 🎯" - Goal: get them to complete the MOST IMPORTANT first action - Content: - Warm welcome (1 paragraph) - "Here's exactly what to do first:" (1 clear action) - Step-by-step with screenshots/GIF (3 steps max) - Link directly to the action (not to a generic dashboard) - Support: "Stuck? Reply to this email — a human reads every one" **Email 2 — Day 2: "Your First Win"** - Subject: "Did you try [FEATURE]? Here's what happens next..." - Goal: guide to the aha moment - Content: - If they completed Step 1: celebrate and introduce Step 2 - If they haven't: gentle reminder with a different angle - Show an example result: "When [CUSTOMER] did this, they got [RESULT]" - Build excitement for what's coming **Email 3 — Day 5: "You're Not Alone"** - Subject: "People like you get the best results when..." - Goal: social proof + deeper feature discovery - Content: - Share usage data: "Most successful users do [X] in their first week" - Customer success story: someone who started where they are - Introduce 2-3 features they might not have found - Community: join the [Slack / Discord / Facebook Group / Forum] **Email 4 — Day 10: "Are You Getting Value?"** - Subject: "Quick question about your experience..." - Goal: identify at-risk users + gather feedback - Content: - NPS or satisfaction question (1-click) - "If everything's going great — here's an advanced tip:" - "If you're stuck — here's how to get help:" - Link to help resources, booking a call, or support chat **Email 5 — Day 14: "What's Next?"** - Subject: "You've mastered the basics — here's your next level" - Goal: transition from onboarding to engagement (and soft upsell) - Content: - Celebrate their progress: "You've done [X] so far — that's great!" - Show what's possible: advanced use cases or features - Introduce the next tier/upsell naturally - Invite to join the community or attend a training FOR EACH EMAIL: | Email | Main CTA | Success Metric | Fallback (if no action) | - Primary call-to-action (one per email) - Tracking: open rate, click rate, action completion - Segmentation: different paths for active vs. inactive users CONSTRAINTS: - Each email must have exactly ONE primary CTA — don't overwhelm new users - The first email must get them to take action in under 2 minutes - Do NOT sell in the first 3 emails — onboard first, sell later - Do NOT assume they remember everything from the purchase page — re-explain value - Subject lines must be mobile-friendly (under 40 characters) - Every email must include easy access to support (reply, link, or chat)
