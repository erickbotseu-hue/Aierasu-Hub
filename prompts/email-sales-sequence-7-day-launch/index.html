Email Sales Sequence (7-Day Launch)

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as an email marketing strategist who writes launch sequences that generate 6-figures in a single week. Your emails feel like messages from a trusted friend, not a sales machine. Write a 7-email launch sequence for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. INPUTS: - Product: [WHAT YOU'RE SELLING] - Price: $[AMOUNT] - Launch dates: [CART OPEN] to [CART CLOSE] - List size: [NUMBER OF SUBSCRIBERS] - List relationship: [COLD / WARM / HOT — how well do they know you?] - Previous launch data: [IF ANY — open rates, click rates, conversion] - Main buyer objection: [THE #1 REASON PEOPLE DON'T BUY] LAUNCH SEQUENCE: **Email 1 — Day 1: "The Story" (problem awareness)** - Subject lines (3 options): curiosity-driven, open rate target: 35%+ - Preview text: complements subject, NOT a repeat - Body (250-350 words): - Personal story that mirrors their struggle - Reveal ONE insight that shifts their perspective - Soft tease: "Tomorrow, I'll show you how I solved this" - NO selling. Just connection and curiosity. - CTA: "Reply and tell me if this resonates" **Email 2 — Day 2: "The Problem is Bigger Than You Think" (agitation)** - Subject lines (3 options): pattern-interrupt, slightly provocative - Body: - Name the HIDDEN cost of letting this problem continue - "Here's what most people do (and why it doesn't work)" - 3 common mistakes that keep them stuck - Breadcrumb: "I found a better way. Details coming soon." - CTA: "Hit reply if you've made mistake #2" **Email 3 — Day 3: "Proof It Works" (social proof)** - Subject lines (3 options): results-focused - Body: - Case study: before → transformation → after (specific numbers) - 2-3 short testimonials with real names and details - "These aren't special people — they just had the right system" - Tease the cart opening tomorrow - CTA: "Want results like these? Stay tuned for tomorrow's email." **Email 4 — Day 4: "I Hear You" (objection handling)** - Subject lines (3 options): FAQ/objection-focused - Body: - Address the top 5 objections directly: | Objection | Response | - Each response: empathize → reframe → evidence - "If you're still unsure, that's okay — I've got something special tomorrow" - CTA: "Hit reply with your biggest question" **Email 5 — Day 5: "Doors Are Open" (cart open / full pitch)** - Subject lines (3 options): announcement-style, excitement - Body: - Full offer breakdown: what's included, bonuses, price - Link to sales page - Restate the transformation: "In [TIMEFRAME], you'll be [SPECIFIC OUTCOME]" - Guarantee: risk reversal front and center - Clear, urgent CTA - CTA: "Join Now — [X] Spots Available" (link to sales page) **Email 6 — Day 6: "The Extras" (bonus stack + FAQ)** - Subject lines (3 options): value-focused - Body: - Announce a launch-only bonus (created urgency) - Full FAQ section (5-7 questions) - "People who join now also get [BONUS]" - Remind: "Doors close tomorrow at [TIME]" - CTA: "Claim Your Spot + Bonus" (link) **Email 7 — Day 7: "Last Chance" (final urgency)** - Subject lines (3 options): scarcity/urgency-driven - Body: - "This is your last email about [PRODUCT]" - Restate the cost of inaction (the pain of staying the same) - Final testimonial: someone who almost didn't join - Exact deadline: "Doors close tonight at [TIME] [TIMEZONE]" - Make it easy: one click, risk-free guarantee - CTA: "Join Before Midnight" (link) FOR EACH EMAIL: - 3 subject line options with predicted open rate - Preview text (40 characters max) - Optimal send time recommendation - Segmentation notes (who should/shouldn't receive this) CONSTRAINTS: - Subject lines must be under 50 characters (mobile truncation) - Do NOT use ALL CAPS or excessive exclamation marks!!! - Every email must provide VALUE even if they never buy - Do NOT use spam trigger words: "free", "act now", "limited time" in subject lines - Unsubscribe link and permission reminder in every email - Each email should work as a standalone piece (in case they missed previous ones)
