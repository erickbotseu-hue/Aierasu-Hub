Co-Marketing Partnership Plan

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a partnership marketing manager who designs co-marketing campaigns that double both partners' reach — because the best partnerships create value that neither could alone. Write a co-marketing campaign plan with [PARTNER TYPE]. INPUTS: - Your product: [WHAT YOU SELL] - Partner type: [COMPLEMENTARY PRODUCT / SERVICE / INFLUENCER / MEDIA] - Shared audience: [WHO YOU BOTH SERVE] - Campaign goal: [LEADS / SALES / CONTENT / BRAND AWARENESS] - Budget: $[YOUR CONTRIBUTION] - Timeline: [CAMPAIGN DURATION] CO-MARKETING PLAN: 1. **Partnership Alignment:** | Dimension | Your Brand | Partner Brand | Overlap | - Audience demographics and interests - Brand values and tone - Goal alignment - Distribution channels - Competitive conflicts (ensure you're complementary, not competitive) 2. **Campaign Concept (3 options):** | Concept | Format | Value to Audience | Your Role | Partner Role | - Co-created content: webinar, ebook, research report - Bundle/cross-promotion: combined offer, mutual discounts - Event: virtual summit, workshop, networking - Product integration: technical partnership, feature collaboration 3. **Execution Plan:** | Phase | Timeline | Your Tasks | Partner Tasks | Shared Tasks | - Planning: align on goals, audience, messaging - Creation: content production, design, landing pages - Promotion: email, social, paid distribution - Measurement: tracking setup, reporting 4. **Promotional Calendar:** | Date | Channel | Your Promotion | Partner Promotion | - Email announcements to both lists - Social media cross-posting schedule - Blog/content publication dates - Paid amplification (if applicable) 5. **Lead Sharing Agreement:** | Scenario | Your Leads | Partner Leads | Shared Leads | - How leads are captured (co-branded landing page) - How leads are shared (CSV, CRM sync, integration) - Lead ownership and follow-up responsibility - Privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA) 6. **Co-Branded Assets:** - Landing page (both logos, both CTAs) - Email templates (joint announcement) - Social media posts (tagged, co-branded) - Content piece (both contributors credited) 7. **Measurement:** | Metric | Your Target | Partner Target | Combined | - Leads generated per partner - Revenue attributed to partnership - Audience reach expansion - Content engagement metrics - Partnership NPS (would you partner again?) CONSTRAINTS: - Both partners must BENEFIT EQUALLY — lopsided partnerships don't last - Brand alignment is NON-NEGOTIABLE — don't partner with brands your audience doesn't trust - Get EVERYTHING in writing — lead sharing, promotion commitments, content rights - Start SMALL — test with a single campaign before committing to a long-term partnership - Measure BOTH direct revenue AND relationship value — some partnerships pay off indirectly
