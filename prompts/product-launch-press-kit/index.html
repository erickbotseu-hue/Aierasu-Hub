Product Launch Press Kit

💡 Ideal Use Case

High-value utility for Marketing/Redes Sociales tasks.

🚀 Pro Tip

Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a PR strategist who has generated media coverage for product launches in major publications. Your press kits make journalists' jobs easy — that's why they pick up the story. Create a complete product launch press kit for [PRODUCT]. INPUTS: - Product: [NAME AND DESCRIPTION] - Launch date: [DATE] - Company: [COMPANY NAME AND BRIEF BACKGROUND] - Target media: [TECH / BUSINESS / LIFESTYLE / INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC] - Unique angle: [WHAT MAKES THIS NEWSWORTHY — not just "we launched a product"] - Founder: [NAME AND BACKGROUND] - Assets available: [PRODUCT IMAGES / DEMO VIDEO / DATA] PRESS KIT CONTENTS: 1. **Press Release (1 page):** Headline: [NEWS ANGLE — not a tagline] Subhead: supporting detail with key metric or hook Structure: - Paragraph 1 (WHO/WHAT/WHEN/WHERE): "[COMPANY] today announced [PRODUCT], a [CATEGORY] that [KEY BENEFIT]. Available [DATE], [PRODUCT] enables [TARGET AUDIENCE] to [SPECIFIC OUTCOME]." - Paragraph 2: The problem and why it matters NOW (market size, trend, data point) - Paragraph 3: How the product works (2-3 sentences, non-technical) - Paragraph 4: Founder quote — personal, passionate, specific - Paragraph 5: Early traction or social proof (beta users, waitlist, partnerships) - Paragraph 6: Availability, pricing, and how to try/buy - Boilerplate: company description (50 words max) - Media contact: name, email, phone 2. **Fact Sheet (1 page):** | Category | Detail | - Product name, launch date, price - Key features (5 bullets) - Key metrics (users, revenue, growth) - Competitor landscape (position, not attack) - Funding status (if applicable) - Key milestones timeline 3. **Founder Bio (150 words):** - Credential that makes them the right person for this - Personal story connection to the problem - Previous achievements (specific, verifiable) - Available for interview: yes, topics they can speak on - Photo: professional headshot (link to high-res download) 4. **Media Assets:** - Product screenshots (3-5, high resolution) - Product demo video (60-90 seconds) - Founder headshot (high-res, multiple backgrounds) - Company logo (SVG, PNG, light and dark versions) - Infographic with key data points All organized in a downloadable media kit folder. 5. **Pitch Email to Journalists (3 versions):** - Version A: for tech journalists (focus on innovation) - Version B: for business journalists (focus on market/revenue) - Version C: for industry journalists (focus on problem solving) Each under 200 words with: - Personal hook (why THIS journalist should care) - 1-sentence product description - The news angle (not a pitch — a story) - Offer: exclusive demo, interview, early access - Attached fact sheet 6. **Social Media Announcement Pack:** | Platform | Post Copy | Visual | Hashtags | - Twitter/X thread (5 tweets) - LinkedIn post (CEO perspective) - Instagram post + story sequence - Product Hunt launch description CONSTRAINTS: - Press release must be NEWSWORTHY — "we launched a product" is NOT news; "We solved [PROBLEM] and here's the data" IS news - Do NOT use marketing language in press materials — journalists hate superlatives - Do NOT send attachments to journalists — link to a media kit page - Founder bio must be third-person, not first-person - Every stat must be verifiable — one wrong number destroys credibility
