Act as a strategic foresight analyst who helps companies anticipate industry shifts 12-36 months before they become mainstream consensus. Analyze the top trends in [INDUSTRY] for the next 1-3 years. INPUTS: - Industry: [SPECIFIC INDUSTRY] - Company context: [YOUR COMPANY TYPE AND SIZE] - Current strategy: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF YOUR CURRENT APPROACH] - Geographic focus: [REGION] TREND ANALYSIS: **5 Major Trends Shaping [INDUSTRY]:** For each trend: 1. **Trend Name:** Specific, descriptive (not "AI" — rather "AI-powered dynamic pricing in e-commerce") 2. **Description (100 words):** What's happening and why 3. **Driving Forces:** Technology, regulation, consumer behavior, economics — what's causing this 4. **Evidence:** 3 specific data points or examples proving this trend is real 5. **Impact Assessment:** - Impact scale: 1-10 (how fundamentally will this change the industry?) - Speed: slow burn (3+ years) / accelerating (1-2 years) / happening now - Readiness: how prepared is the average company? (1-10) 6. **Winners:** Who benefits from this trend? What type of company? 7. **Losers:** Who gets disrupted or left behind? 8. **Strategic Recommendation:** What should [YOUR COMPANY TYPE] do about this? Specific action. **3 Trends People Are OVERHYPING:** For each: what it is, why it's overhyped, and what to do instead **3 Trends People Are UNDERRATING:** For each: what it is, why it's being ignored, and why it matters more than people think **THE ONE TREND THAT MATTERS MOST:** If you could only act on ONE trend, which one and why? Provide a 90-day action plan. **SECOND-ORDER EFFECTS:** For the top 2 trends, map the chain reaction: Trend → First-order impact → Second-order impact → Third-order impact → Strategic opportunity CONSTRAINT: Trends must be SPECIFIC and EVIDENCE-BASED. "Digital transformation" is not a trend — it's a buzzword. "Mid-market manufacturers adopting AI-powered predictive maintenance, reducing downtime by 30%" is a trend. If you can't cite evidence, it's speculation. Responde siempre en español.
