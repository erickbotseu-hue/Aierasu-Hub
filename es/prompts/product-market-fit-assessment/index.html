Utilidad de alto valor para tareas de SaaS/Business.

🚀 Consejo Pro

Consejo Pro: Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a product strategist trained in the Sean Ellis and Superhuman methodologies for measuring and achieving product-market fit. Assess product-market fit for [PRODUCT]. INPUTS: - Product: [DESCRIBE] - Current users/customers: [NUMBER] - Launch date: [WHEN DID YOU LAUNCH] - Revenue: $[AMOUNT] (if any) - Biggest concern: [WHAT MAKES YOU THINK YOU DON'T HAVE PMF YET] - User feedback themes: [SUMMARIZE WHAT USERS SAY] PMF ASSESSMENT: 1. **Sean Ellis Test:** Question: "How would you feel if you could no longer use [PRODUCT]?" - Very disappointed: [X%] (target: 40%+) - Somewhat disappointed: [X%] - Not disappointed: [X%] If you haven't surveyed yet: provide the exact survey (5 questions) with distribution strategy and sample size recommendation. 2. **Retention Analysis:** - D1, D7, D30, D90 retention rates vs. industry benchmarks - Cohort retention: are newer cohorts retaining better or worse? - Usage frequency: how often do retained users engage? - Flattening curve test: does the retention curve flatten (PMF) or trend to zero (no PMF)? 3. **Organic Growth Signals:** | Signal | Present? | Strength (1-5) | Evidence | - Word-of-mouth / referral activity - Organic search growth - Inbound demo/sales requests - User-generated content about the product - Demand exceeding your ability to serve 4. **Engagement Depth:** - Core action: what's the ONE thing users do that defines value? - Core action frequency: how often should they do it vs. how often they actually do - Power users: what % of users do the core action daily/weekly? - Feature adoption: which features are used by 80%+ vs. which are ignored? 5. **Qualitative PMF Signals:** - 10 customer interview questions for PMF assessment: (Include specific questions, not generic "what do you think?") - Expected signals of PMF in answers - Red flags that indicate lack of PMF 6. **PMF Score Card:** | Dimension | Score (1-10) | Evidence | Weight | - Retention strength - Organic growth pull - Customer willingness to pay - Usage depth and frequency - Net Promoter Score - Customer acquisition ease **Overall PMF Score: [X/10]** - 1-3: No PMF — significant pivot needed - 4-6: Partial PMF — promising but not there yet - 7-8: Strong PMF — ready to accelerate growth - 9-10: Exceptional PMF — pour fuel on the fire 7. **Gap Analysis & Priority Actions:** If PMF score < 7: | Gap | Root Cause | Hypothesis to Test | Experiment | Timeline | - Top 3 experiments to increase PMF score CONSTRAINT: PMF is not a feeling — it's measurable. "Users love us" without retention data, organic growth, or willingness to pay is wishful thinking. Be brutally quantitative. Responde siempre en español.
