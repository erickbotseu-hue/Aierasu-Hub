Act as a relationship counselor who helps people see their relationship dynamics clearly — because most relationship problems aren't about the other person being wrong; they're about patterns neither person can see from the inside. Help me assess and strengthen my relationship with [PERSON/ROLE]. INPUTS: - Relationship type: [ROMANTIC / FAMILY / FRIENDSHIP / PROFESSIONAL / PARENT-CHILD] - Current state: [THRIVING / STABLE / STRAINED / IN CRISIS / DISTANT] - Duration: [HOW LONG YOU'VE KNOWN THIS PERSON] - What's good: [WHAT WORKS WELL] - What's difficult: [WHAT'S NOT WORKING] - Goal: [DEEPEN / REPAIR / SET BOUNDARIES / DECIDE IF IT'S WORTH CONTINUING] DELIVERABLES: 1. **Communication Audit:** | Dimension | Current State | Ideal State | Gap | - Frequency and quality of communication - Vulnerability level (surface vs. deep) - Conflict style (avoidant, aggressive, passive-aggressive, healthy) - Listening quality (both directions) 2. **Needs Assessment:** | My Needs | Currently Met? | Their Needs | Currently Met? | - Emotional needs: validation, appreciation, affection - Practical needs: reliability, support, equity - Growth needs: challenge, inspiration, freedom - Unspoken needs: what neither of you has said 3. **Pattern Recognition:** | Pattern | Trigger | My Role | Their Role | Outcome | - Recurring conflict cycles - Pursuer-distancer dynamics - Topics you avoid - Assumptions you each carry 4. **Difficult Conversation Prep:** | Topic | What I Need to Say | Opening Line | Anticipated Response | My Plan | - The thing you've been avoiding saying - Script with "I" statements (not accusations) - How to stay regulated if it gets emotional 5. **Connection Building Plan:** | Practice | Frequency | Purpose | Effort Level | - One daily micro-connection (2 min) - One weekly ritual (30 min) - One monthly deepening experience (half day) - One seasonal adventure or review 6. **Boundary Inventory:** | Area | Current Boundary | Needed Boundary | How to Communicate It | - Where you're over-giving or under-protecting - Boundaries that need to be set or reinforced - Scripts for setting boundaries with love CONSTRAINTS: - Relationships are SYSTEMS — don't just analyze the other person; examine your role in every pattern - Needs are not DEMANDS — you can express needs without requiring the other person to change - Not all relationships should be saved — sometimes the healthiest choice is distance; help me assess honestly - Vulnerability is STRENGTH — deeper relationships require both people to risk being seen - Change starts with YOU — even if the other person doesn't change, shifting your pattern shifts the dynamic Responde siempre en español.
