Product Demo Script

💡 Caso de Uso Ideal

Utilidad de alto valor para tareas de Marketing/Redes Sociales.

🚀 Consejo Pro

Consejo Pro: Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a product marketing specialist who creates demo scripts that showcase value, not features — because the best demo makes the viewer think "I need this NOW." Write a product demo script for [PRODUCT] (15 minutes). INPUTS: - Product: [WHAT IT IS AND WHAT IT DOES] - Target audience: [WHO IS WATCHING THE DEMO] - Demo context: [LIVE CALL / RECORDED VIDEO / WEBINAR / IN-APP TOUR] - Key use case: [THE #1 PROBLEM IT SOLVES] - Competitor comparison: [WHAT MAKES THIS DEMO DIFFERENT THAN COMPETITOR DEMOS] - Aha moment: [THE FEATURE/MOMENT THAT MAKES PEOPLE'S EYES LIGHT UP] DEMO SCRIPT: 1. **Opening Hook (0:00-1:00):** Don't start with "Let me show you our product..." START with: "Imagine you're [SCENARIO]... and instead of [OLD WAY], you could [NEW WAY]..." or: "What if I showed you how [SIMILAR COMPANY] went from [BEFORE] to [AFTER] in [TIME]?" 2. **Problem Setup (1:00-3:00):** - Show the OLD way: manual, frustrating, time-consuming - "This is what most [AUDIENCE] are doing right now..." - Quantify the pain: "This costs you [X HOURS / $X / Y OPPORTUNITIES] per month" - "There has to be a better way... and there is." 3. **Product Reveal (3:00-4:00):** - First impression: clean dashboard/interface - One-sentence positioning: "[PRODUCT] is [WHAT IT IS] that [KEY BENEFIT]" - "Let me show you exactly how it works..." 4. **Live Walkthrough (4:00-11:00):** Show 3 key features, connected as a STORY (not a feature tour): | Segment | Feature | User Story | Time | - Feature 1 (3 min): the core value — the one thing they NEED - Set up the scenario: "Let's say you need to [TASK]..." - Show it live: [CLICK-BY-CLICK NARRATION] - Highlight the moment: "Did you see that? What took [X HOURS] now takes [Y SECONDS]" - Feature 2 (2 min): the multiplier — makes Feature 1 even better - "But it gets better..." - Show a capability competitors don't have - Feature 3 (2 min): the aha moment — the thing they didn't know they needed - "Here's what really sets us apart..." - This should be the emotional peak of the demo 5. **Results / Social Proof (11:00-12:00):** - "[COMPANY] used this exact workflow and saw [SPECIFIC RESULT]" - Show real data or before/after screenshots - "And they're not alone — [X]+ teams use [PRODUCT] to [OUTCOME]" 6. **Close (12:00-15:00):** - Summarize what they saw: "In the last 12 minutes, you saw how to [1], [2], and [3]" - CTA based on context: - Live demo: "Want to try this with YOUR data? Let's set you up." - Recorded video: "Start your free trial at [URL]" - Webinar: "Special offer for attendees — [DEAL]" - Q&A (if live): prepare answers for top 5 expected questions DEMO DELIVERY TIPS: - Pace: slightly slower than normal conversation (processing time) - Pauses: after key reveals, let it sink in (2-3 seconds) - Cursor: use smooth movements, highlight with circles/annotations - Energy: match the viewer's energy level (enthusiastic for marketing, calm for enterprise) CONSTRAINTS: - Do NOT show every feature — show the 3 that solve THEIR problem - Do NOT go longer than 15 minutes — attention spans are short - Do NOT use dummy data — use realistic scenarios they relate to - Do NOT talk about features — talk about outcomes: "You can X" not "It has X" - ALWAYS end with a clear next step — never end with "any questions?"
