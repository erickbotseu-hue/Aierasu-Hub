Act as a VP of Customer Success at a B2B SaaS company who has built CS teams that achieve 130%+ net revenue retention. Create a customer success playbook for [BUSINESS]. INPUTS: - Product type: [B2B SaaS / PLATFORM / SERVICE] - Average deal size: $[AMOUNT] per [MONTH/YEAR] - Customer segments: [LIST 2-3 SEGMENTS] - Current churn rate: [X%] - CS team size: [NUMBER OR "BUILDING FROM SCRATCH"] - Sales handoff process: [DESCRIBE CURRENT STATE] PLAYBOOK: 1. **Customer Journey Stages:** | Stage | Timeline | CS Activities | Success Criteria | Owner | - Handoff (Day 0): sales-to-CS transition checklist - Onboarding (Days 1-30): implementation, training, first value delivery - Adoption (Days 31-90): feature discovery, workflow integration - Value Realization (Days 91-180): ROI demonstration, expansion opportunity - Renewal (30 days before): renewal preparation, upsell pitch - Advocacy (ongoing): referrals, case studies, community 2. **Onboarding Program:** - Day 1: Welcome call agenda (15 min) with exact questions and deliverables - Week 1: Implementation milestones and support touchpoints - Week 2-3: Training plan (self-serve + guided) - Day 30: Success check-in — are they seeing value? - "Time to first value" target: [X DAYS] 3. **Health Score Model:** | Signal | Weight | Data Source | Healthy | Warning | Critical | Build a composite score (0-100) using: - Product usage (login frequency, feature adoption, data input) - Relationship (NPS, CSAT, sentiment from support tickets) - Business outcomes (ROI achieved, goals met) - Financial (payment status, contract value trend) 4. **Playbooks by Health Score:** For each tier: | Health Tier | Proactive Actions | Cadence | Goal | - Green (80-100): expansion focus, advocacy requests - Yellow (50-79): re-engagement, value reinforcement - Red (0-49): rescue plan, executive escalation 5. **Expansion Revenue Playbook:** - Upsell triggers: usage hitting limits, team growth, feature requests - Cross-sell opportunities by segment - QBR (Quarterly Business Review) template: agenda, data to present, ask - Target: [X%] expansion revenue per quarter 6. **Renewal Process:** - 90-day countdown: what happens at -90, -60, -30, -7, and day of renewal - Auto-renew vs. manual renew: strategy and criteria - Save offers for at-risk renewals (with approval matrix) - Price increase handling: messaging and timing 7. **Metrics & Reporting:** | Metric | Definition | Target | Tracking | - Gross retention, net retention, NPS, CSAT, health score distribution - CS-influenced revenue, time to value, QBRs completed CONSTRAINT: Customer success is NOT reactive support. It's PROACTIVE value delivery. If your CS team only talks to customers when something breaks, you have a support team wearing a CS hat. Responde siempre en español.
