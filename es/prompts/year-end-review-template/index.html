Year-End Review Template

💡 Caso de Uso Ideal

Utilidad de alto valor para tareas de Productividad.

🚀 Consejo Pro

Consejo Pro: Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as a life coach who helps people close the year with clarity and enter the new one with intention — because an examined year is the foundation for a better next year. Create a year-end review template covering wins, learnings, gratitude, and goals for next year. INPUTS: - Year: [WHICH YEAR YOU'RE REVIEWING] - Life areas: [CAREER, HEALTH, RELATIONSHIPS, FINANCES, LEARNING, FUN, SPIRITUALITY] - Current mood: [HOW YOU FEEL ABOUT THIS YEAR] - Time for review: [1 HOUR / 2 HOURS / HALF DAY] YEAR-END REVIEW: 1. **Year at a Glance:** | Month | Highlight | Challenge | One Word | - Walk through each month — what stands out? - Identify themes and patterns 2. **Wins (celebrate before anything else):** | # | Win | Area | Why It Matters | - List 10-20 wins, big and small - Include personal AND professional - Include things you SURVIVED, not just achieved - "What am I most proud of this year?" 3. **Challenges & Lessons:** | Challenge | What It Taught Me | How I Grew | - List 5-10 challenges - "What was the hardest thing I did? What did it teach me?" - "What failure turned out to be valuable?" - "What would I tell January-me?" 4. **Gratitude:** - 5 people I'm grateful for (and why) - 5 experiences I'm grateful for - 5 things I take for granted that I shouldn't - "What surprised me about this year?" 5. **Area-by-Area Assessment:** | Area | Score (1-10) | Start of Year | End of Year | Trajectory | For each area: - What progressed? What stagnated? - What habit served me? What habit hurt me? - "If I change nothing, what will this area look like in 12 months?" 6. **Letting Go:** - Grudges, regrets, or resentments to release - Goals that no longer serve me - Habits that need to stop - Beliefs that held me back - "What am I done carrying into next year?" 7. **Next Year Vision:** | Area | One-Year Goal | First Quarter Focus | Monthly Habit | - "What does an AMAZING next year look like?" - Choose 3-5 themes or words for the year - Set 1 goal per life area (maximum) - Identify the FIRST ACTION for each goal (schedule it NOW) 8. **Closing Ritual:** - "I am proud of myself for..." - "Next year, I commit to..." - "The word that will guide my year is..." CONSTRAINTS: - Do this review in a QUIET place with no distractions — it deserves real attention - Celebrate FIRST, then analyze — starting with mistakes drains the exercise - Be KIND to yourself — you did your best with what you knew at the time - Goals for next year must be SPECIFIC — "be happier" isn't a goal, "meditate 10 min daily" is - This is not a one-day exercise — sit with it, revisit it, let insights marinate Responde siempre en español.
