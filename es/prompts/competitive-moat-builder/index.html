Analyze and strengthen the competitive moat for [COMPANY]. INPUTS: - Company: [NAME AND DESCRIPTION] - Industry: [INDUSTRY] - Company age: [YEARS] - Revenue: $[AMOUNT] - Key competitors: [LIST 3-5] - What you think your advantage is: [YOUR CURRENT BELIEF] MOAT ANALYSIS: **7 Moat Types Assessment:** | Moat Type | Score (1-10) | Current Evidence | Improvement Potential | 1. **Network Effects:** - Does the product become MORE valuable as more people use it? - Type: direct (more users = more value to each user) or indirect (more users attract more suppliers)? - Strength test: if a competitor launched with better features but zero users, would your users switch? - Current score and specific evidence 2. **Switching Costs:** - How painful is it for a customer to leave? - Types: financial, procedural (learning curve), relational (human relationships) - Data lock-in: do customers store valuable data in your system? - Integration depth: how many other tools connect to yours? - Current score and specific evidence 3. **Brand & Reputation:** - Brand recognition: do people in your market know your name? - Brand preference: do they choose you over alternatives? - Brand premium: can you charge more because of brand alone? - Trust signals: reviews, testimonials, press, awards - Current score and specific evidence 4. **Scale Economies:** - Does your cost per unit decrease as you grow? - Fixed costs that are spread over more customers - Purchasing power with suppliers - Distribution economies - Current score and specific evidence 5. **Data & Algorithmic Advantage:** - Do you have unique data that competitors can't access? - Does your product improve with more data (machine learning, recommendations)? - First-mover data advantage: are you accumulating data faster? - Current score and specific evidence 6. **Intellectual Property:** - Patents, trade secrets, proprietary processes - Copyrighted content or unique methodologies - Regulatory approvals (FDA, FCC, etc.) - Current score and specific evidence 7. **Regulatory / Legal Moat:** - Licenses or certifications required (barriers to competitors) - Government contracts or exclusive agreements - Compliance complexity that deters new entrants - Current score and specific evidence **OVERALL MOAT ASSESSMENT:** - Total moat score: [X/70] - Moat strength: None (0-15) / Narrow (16-35) / Wide (36-55) / Fortress (56-70) - Primary moat source: [which type is strongest] - Most vulnerable point: [which moat is weakest] **5 SPECIFIC ACTIONS TO DEEPEN MOAT:** | Action | Moat Type Strengthened | Investment | Timeline | Expected Impact | **MOAT EVOLUTION ROADMAP:** | Timeline | Current Moat | Deepening Actions | Target Moat | - 6 months: quick wins - 12 months: structural changes - 24 months: unassailable position CONSTRAINT: A moat that can be replicated in 6 months is not a moat — it's a head start.
