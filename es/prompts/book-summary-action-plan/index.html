Book Summary & Action Plan

💡 Caso de Uso Ideal

Utilidad de alto valor para tareas de Educación.

🚀 Consejo Pro

Consejo Pro: Optimiza tus resultados iterando el prompt.

Act as an executive book coach who helps people extract maximum value from nonfiction books — because the purpose of reading isn't to collect highlights, it's to change how you think and act. Help me extract maximum value from [BOOK TITLE] by [AUTHOR]. INPUTS: - Book: [TITLE AND AUTHOR] - Why I read it: [CAREER / PERSONAL GROWTH / SPECIFIC PROBLEM / CURIOSITY] - Key areas of interest: [WHAT I HOPED TO LEARN] - My context: [MY ROLE, SITUATION, OR CHALLENGE] - Notes: [PASTE ANY HIGHLIGHTS OR NOTES — optional] BOOK VALUE EXTRACTION: 1. **Core Thesis (1-2 sentences):** The author's main argument in one breath. 2. **Top 5 Key Ideas:** | # | Idea | Why It Matters | My Reaction | - The 5 ideas that have the most potential to change behavior or thinking - For each: why this matters specifically to YOUR situation - Prioritized by applicability, not by book order 3. **3 Ideas That Challenge My Thinking:** | Idea | My Previous Belief | The Challenge | Resolution | - The most valuable part of a book is where it makes you UNCOMFORTABLE - What did you believe before? What does the book argue? Who's right? 4. **5 Actionable Takeaways:** | # | Action | First Step | Deadline | Expected Impact | - Specific things to DO (not just "think about") - Each with a clear FIRST STEP and TIMELINE - "This week, I will..." 5. **Questions the Book Doesn't Answer:** | Question | Why It Matters | Where to Find the Answer | - What holes does the book leave? - What would you ask the author? - Where do you disagree? 6. **Related Reading:** | Book | Author | Connection | Read Next If... | - 3 books that complement, challenge, or extend this one - "Read [BOOK] if you want to go deeper on [TOPIC]" 7. **30-Day Implementation Plan:** | Week | Action | From Chapter/Idea | Success Metric | - Turn the best ideas into a month-long experiment - Each week: one idea, implemented and tested CONSTRAINTS: - Ideas must be in YOUR OWN WORDS — parroting the author isn't understanding - Actions must be SPECIFIC — "be more productive" isn't actionable; "time-block my mornings" is - Challenge the author — no book is 100% right for your situation - If you can't implement at least 3 actions, the book wasn't worth reading (or you need to re-read it) - Book notes should be ALIVE — review and update them as you implement Responde siempre en español.
