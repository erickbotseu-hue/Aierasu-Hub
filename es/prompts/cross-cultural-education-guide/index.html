Duration: [HOURS/SESSIONS] CULTURAL COMPETENCY TRAINING: 1. **Foundation: Cultural Awareness:** | Concept | Content | Activity | - Culture is an iceberg: visible (food, dress) vs. invisible (values, communication norms) - Your own cultural lens: how YOUR culture shapes your assumptions - Cultural dimensions framework: individualism/collectivism, power distance, etc. 2. **Communication Across Cultures:** | Dimension | Culture A | Culture B | Potential Friction | - Direct vs. indirect communication styles - High-context vs. low-context messaging - Silence, eye contact, and personal space - Written vs. verbal vs. nonverbal 3. **Business/Professional Norms:** | Area | Culture A Norm | Culture B Norm | How to Navigate | - Meeting culture: punctuality, formality, decision-making - Hierarchy and authority: how to address seniors - Feedback: direct critique vs. saving face - Relationship-building: task-first vs. relationship-first 4. **Common Misunderstandings:** | Scenario | What Happened | Cultural Explanation | Better Approach | - Real-world scenarios illustrating cultural friction - "When X did [ACTION], Y interpreted it as [MEANING], but it actually meant [REALITY]" 5. **Building Cultural Intelligence (CQ):** | Component | How to Develop | - CQ Drive: motivation to learn about other cultures - CQ Knowledge: understanding cultural differences - CQ Strategy: planning for cross-cultural encounters - CQ Action: adapting behavior appropriately CONSTRAINTS: - Avoid stereotypes — cultural tendencies are TRENDS, not rules for every individual - Focus on UNDERSTANDING, not judgment — different ≠ wrong - Include self-reflection — understanding YOUR culture is the first step -
