(2 min) - Explain: core concept in simple terms (5 min) - Show: example or demonstration (3 min) - Practice: activity where THEY do something (5 min) - Check: did they get it? (2 min) - Q&A: questions and clarification (3 min) 4. **Assessment:** | Who | What | How | - Learners assess the teacher: clarity, engagement, usefulness - Teacher self-assesses: what went well, what to improve - Facilitator observes: coaching feedback 5. **Benefits Framework:** | Benefit | For Teacher | For Learner | - Deeper understanding through explaining - Peer-level language and relatability - Building communication and leadership skills - Diverse perspectives on the material CONSTRAINTS: - The TEACHER learns more than the student — this is a feature, not a bug - Peer teaching requires STRUCTURE — unstructured "just teach each other" produces chaos - Train peer teachers — don't assume teaching skills come naturally - Evaluate gently — the goal is growth, not perfection - Rotate teaching roles — everyone should teach AND learn Responde siempre en español.
