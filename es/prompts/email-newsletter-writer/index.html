Write a weekly email newsletter edition for: - Topic: [TOPIC] - Audience: [DESCRIBE READER — their role, challenges, goals] - Newsletter name: [NAME OR "suggest one"] - Brand voice: [witty / professional / nerdy / warm and casual] DELIVER: 1. **Subject Lines (3 options):** - Option A: Curiosity-based (open loop — they MUST know the answer) - Option B: Benefit-based (specific outcome for the reader) - Option C: Urgency/timely (connected to current events or deadline) Rate each 1-10 for predicted open rate, explain your favorite. 2. **Preview Text:** 40-90 characters that complements (not repeats) the subject line 3. **Email Body:** - **Personal opener (2-3 sentences):** Relatable observation, mini-story, or confession that connects to the topic.
